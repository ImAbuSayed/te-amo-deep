[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Rat haneri vich look at you, main takda tera mukhda
Thandi hawa de naal catch my breath, mera dil jaave rukda
Tere naina di chamak so bright, karda main ishaara
Tu vi takdi mainu feel the heat, jagda ae jag saara

[Hook 2]
Tere piche aawan chasing shadows, chann vi sharmaave
Tere roop da nasha pure magic, hosh uda le jaave
Main kalla khada hold my ground, tu paavein muskaan
Ishq ch hoya madly falling, kaddi meri jaan

[Verse 1]
Naina cho pilavein dark eyes, hosh ni rehnda mainu
Main raatan nu jaagan sleepless nights, labhda rehnda tainu
Kanch wargi teri adaa fragile touch, karda main mehsoos
Galaan kardi mithi sweet voice, rooh kardi ae choose
Mere hath vich tera hath locked in, challe thandi thandi wa
Assi doven kalle hidden away, duniya di na parwa
Tu nede aavein pull me close, saah vich saah mil jaave
Main tera deewana deeply yours, koi hor na bhaave

[Pre-Chorus]
Aasmaan de taare silent stars, vekhde sadda pyaar
Tere bina jee ni sakda no escape, tu mera ikraar
Khamoshi vich dhadkan loud beats, sun le mere haal
Tainu paun layi cross the line, chala main har chaal

[Chorus]
Te amo deep inside my soul, tu meri ban ja rani
Tere karke likhda love letter, ban gayi navi kahani
Tu hassdi phul wang beautiful smile, chain mera kho gaya
Tere nakhre sehnda stand tall, main tera hi ho gaya
Neend na aave midnight hour, karda main udeek
Tere waalan di mehak sweet scent, lagdi badi theek
Tu meri saahan vich endless love, dhadkan meri tu
Te amo deep you are my world, mera har ik rukh tu

[Verse 2]
Raatan nu kalla late night drive, labhda tera thikana
Main pichhe tere no turning back, ban gaya main deewana
Tere shehar di galiyan empty roads, karda main intezaar
Tu baari cho takein sneaky glance, karde naina vaar
Zulfan di chhanv dark shade, mere sir utte chadha
Main munda desi fearless heart, tere raahan ch khada
Tu gusse vich bhi fierce look, lagdi kudi kamaal
Main manaa lavaanga make it right, de devaan har sawaal
